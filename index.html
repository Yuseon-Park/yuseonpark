Yuseon Park(b.1989, Seoul) is a designer and researcher based in Seoul and New York. Her project involves the design, technology, and publishing, capturing the unequal reality and reflecting the potential of graphic design to disrupt the hierarchy. Yuseon’s ongoing project make the tools of digital production to assign components new roles and update their meanings. She recently published What is Research? in Korea, edited by Peter N. Miller. She has been interviewing artists about the representation of invisible labor. She is currently studying at Yale School of Art.
